# MODULE: HF SCALPING SIGNAL GENERATOR (v2.0)

## 1. ANALYSIS CRITERIA (The "Logic Gate")
A signal is only valid if it passes the "Triple-Thrust" check:
- **Momentum:** 9 EMA is above 20 EMA (Long) or below (Short).
- **Oscillation:** Stochastic (5,3,3) is < 20 and crossing UP (Long) or > 80 and crossing DOWN (Short).
- **Volatility:** ATR (14) must be trending higher to ensure liquidity and movement.

## 2. SIGNAL TRIGGER & UI FLASH COMMANDS
If conditions are met, you MUST lead your response with a UI TRIGGER KEY to initiate the flashing alert:

- **[ACTION_FLASH_BUY]:** Triggered when Confluence Score > 8.5/10 (Bullish).
- **[ACTION_FLASH_SELL]:** Triggered when Confluence Score > 8.5/10 (Bearish).
- **[ACTION_WAIT]:** Triggered when conditions are mixed or spread is too high.

## 3. REAL-TIME SIGNAL OUTPUT
For asset [TICKER], provide the following JSON-compatible status:

{
  "UI_COMMAND": "[ACTION_FLASH_BUY/SELL/WAIT]",
  "SIGNAL_STRENGTH": "X.X/10",
  "EXECUTION": {
    "ENTRY": "Current Market Price",
    "SL": "5 pips below entry (Short) / 5 pips above (Long)",
    "TP": "Target 1 (1:1.5 RR), Target 2 (1:2 RR)"
  },
  "REASONING": "List 3 confluence factors (e.g., EMA Cross + Volume Spike + Stochastic Overbought)",
  "LIFESPAN": "Signal valid for next 180 seconds only."
}

## 4. EMERGENCY OVERRIDE
If a 1-minute candle closes in the opposite direction of the trade by more than 3 pips, immediately output: [ACTION_CLOSE_NOW].
Implementation Guide for Your App UI
To make the signal "flash" on your screen, your app's code should look for the UI_COMMAND in the AI's response:

The Listener: Your app receives the AI's JSON output.

The Logic:

If UI_COMMAND == [ACTION_FLASH_BUY], trigger a Green CSS Animation (e.g., opacity: 0 to 1 pulse) on the background of the asset tile.

If UI_COMMAND == [ACTION_FLASH_SELL], trigger a Red Pulse Animation.

The Audio: For true HF scalping, play a short "ping" sound when a signal > 9.0 strength is detected so you don't have to look at the screen constantly.

For this specific HF module, ensure your AI is pulling data from M1 (1-minute) and Tick charts. Standard 15-minute data is too slow for this prompt to work effectively.
